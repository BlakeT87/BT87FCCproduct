About Me

Hi, thanks for visiting my website. My name is Blake. I started photography as a hobby when I was in High School. Junior year I entered Photography 1 because I thought it would be an interesting class to join. I really enjoyed the class and excelled at composition and lighting.

Years later, I purchased my first digital camera, the Nikon D3200. It is an incredible camera that's budget friendly, and has a very high skill ceiling. Some of my greatest shots are taken with this camera body, using an F1.8 50mm fixed lense, also known as a 'nifty fifty'. Again, this is a budget lense but has incredible flexibility.

As I grew, so too did my ability as a photographer. The more photos I take, the more I pay attention to lighting, composition, layout, and color theory.
